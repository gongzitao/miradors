Frequently Asked Questions

What is Miradors?

Miradors is an AI-powered tour assistant app that helps you explore food and sights by analyzing photos. Simply take a picture of a menu, dish, or landmark, and get instant information, translations, and recommendations.

How do I use Miradors?

  1. Open the app and tap the camera button
  2. Take a photo of a menu, food, or landmark
  3. Get instant AI-powered analysis and recommendations
  4. Ask follow-up questions in the chat

What can I photograph?

  • Restaurant Menus: Get translations and dish recommendations
  • Food & Dishes: Learn about ingredients and cuisine styles
  • Landmarks & Buildings: Discover history and interesting facts
  • Signs & Text: Translate and understand foreign text

Do I need an account?

No! Miradors works without any sign-up or login. Just download and start exploring.

How do I find nearby restaurants?

When you allow location access, Miradors can recommend restaurants near you. You'll see real photos, ratings, distances, and whether they're currently open.

Can I save my discoveries?

Yes! All your chats are automatically saved in your history. You can also save photos you take to your photo library.

Troubleshooting

Camera not working?

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera
  2. Make sure Miradors is enabled
  3. Restart the app

Location features not working?

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  2. Ensure Location Services is on
  3. Find Miradors and select "While Using App"

App not responding?

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Force quit the app and restart
  3. Update to the latest version
  4. If issues persist, contact support
